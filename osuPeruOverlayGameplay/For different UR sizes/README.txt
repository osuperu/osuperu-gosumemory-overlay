This folder is for different UR sizes, copy the one that matches your necessities, paste and replace the old file on the main folder of this overlay

Your UR size is not necessarilly needed to be the ones indicated on the files. It's rather for you to choose the one that's closer to your's.

The sizes indicated on their names are the maximum they are thought for. Though, bigger size may still fit.

Please take in account that UR width changes depending on AR and OD as well.
